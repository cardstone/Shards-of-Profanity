The site.

Shards of Profanity is a browser-based Cards Against Humanity clone by a team of noob undergraduate developers.

The tech.

The webapp is built upon the M.E.A.N. stack (MongoDB, Express, Angular.js, Node.js) and utilizes Socket.IO for the realtime client-server communication.

https://github.com/cardstone/shards-of-profanity

Rules.

There are 2 roles in Shards of Profanity - the player, and the Czar.

The Czar is responsible for starting the round, as well as choosing the winner of the respective round.

The player just selects the card they like the best.

The Game

1. When the round starts, a countdown starts and players must select their cards.

2. When the round ends, the Czar chooses the winner.

Loop.
