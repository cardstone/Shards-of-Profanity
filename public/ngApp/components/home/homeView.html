The site.

Shards of Profanity is a browser-based Cards Against Humanity clone by a team of noob undergraduate developers.

The tech.

The webapp is built upon the M.E.A.N. stack (MongoDB, Express, Angular.js, Node.js) and utilizes Socket.IO for the realtime client-server communication.

https://github.com/cardstone/shards-of-profanity

The roles.

Players can take one of two roles in Shards of Profanity, average shmucks and the almighty Card-Czar (♔). Players take turns being the Czar every round.

The rules.

A new black card is revealed each round. Players submit white card(s) from their hand to complete the statement on the black card. The Card-Czar bestows a point upon the player with the funniest submission. Lulz will be had. Happy Shards of Profanity, may the cards be ever in your favor.
