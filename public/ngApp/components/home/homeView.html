The site.

Shards of Profanity is a browser-based Cards Against Humanity clone by a team of noob developers.

The tech.

The webapp is built upon the M.E.A.N. stack (MongoDB, Express, Angular.js, Node.js) and utilizes Socket.IO for the realtime client-server communication.
