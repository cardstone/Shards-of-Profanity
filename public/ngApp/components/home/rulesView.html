Terminology

Czar: This power can be bestowed on 1 player in a game or round. They will have to power to start rounds, as well as choose the victor of those respective rounds.


Playing
1. The Czar starts the round.
2. A black card appears on the screen.
3. Non-Czars select a white card from their hand that they find the funniest.
4. Once time is up, the Czar picks the funniest card.
